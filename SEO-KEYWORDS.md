# ClinicFlow — SEO & Keyword Strategy

_Last updated: 2026-07-01. Based on competitor on-page SEO analysis + Hebrew keyword research (validated against live SERPs)._

## TL;DR — the whole strategy in three sentences

1. **Every Israeli competitor is cloud + monthly subscription** and brands itself "תוכנת ענן". The search space for **offline / ללא מנוי / תשלום חד-פעמי / תיקון 13** is wide open — nobody is defending it.
2. So we do **not** win by fighting the incumbents head-on for the generic head term "תוכנה לניהול קליניקה" (they have years of authority). We win by **owning the wedge terms** where we are both uniquely true *and* uncontested, and attaching the head term to them.
3. Keywords are necessary but **not sufficient** to rank #1 — see [What keywords alone will NOT fix](#what-keywords-alone-will-not-fix) at the bottom. The single biggest blocker right now is that **the site is gated behind a pre-launch lock and Google cannot index it.**

## What keywords alone will NOT fix

Ranking #1 is keywords **plus** these. In rough order of impact for this site today:

1. **🚨 The pre-launch site lock.** `index.html` redirects every visitor (including Googlebot) to `/soon` unless `localStorage.cf_unlocked === "1"`. **While this is on, Google indexes nothing but the splash page** — the keyword work cannot rank. Removing this gate at launch is prerequisite #1.
2. **Content depth.** Google ranks pages, not meta tags. The landing pages + תיקון 13 guide above are what actually rank for the long-tail. A homepage alone won't cover 40+ keywords.
3. **Backlinks / authority.** Incumbents have years of domain authority. Get links from professional associations (הפ"י, איגוד הפיזיותרפיה, עמותת הדיאטנים), accountants serving clinicians, and niche directories. This is the slowest but most decisive lever for the competitive head terms.
4. **Page speed & Core Web Vitals.** The heavy hero animation (GooeyText morphing, framer-motion) can hurt LCP on mobile. Measure with PageSpeed Insights; lazy-load below-the-fold.
5. **Local/entity signals.** Google Business Profile, consistent NAP, and the brand-disambiguation lockup ("ClinicFlow — ניהול קליניקה אופליין") to separate us from other "clinic flow" entities.
6. **Realistic timeline.** Even done perfectly, new pages take **weeks to months** to rank. There is no same-week #1. Track in Google Search Console.

**Bottom line:** the keyword targeting is now correct and the wedge is uncontested. The gating factors from here are **unlocking the site, publishing the landing-page content, and earning backlinks** — not more keywords.
