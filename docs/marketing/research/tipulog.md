## 1. Snapshot

Tipulog is the self-described **"leading cloud software in Israel for clinic/practice management"** ("תוכנת הענן המובילה בישראל לניהול קליניקה / מרפאה").
- Source: homepage, https://tipulog.co.il/ — accessed 2026-07-12
- Deployment: **cloud / internet-based only** ("תוכנת ענן אינטרנטית"). No offline mode is mentioned; the FAQ lists supported browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari) and requires an internet connection. Source: FAQ page, https://tipulog.co.il/שאלות-נפוצות — accessed 2026-07-12
- Business model: **SaaS monthly subscription** (see pricing below).
- Target users: private therapists, treatment centers, clinics, and physicians/medical practices. Sources: homepage and https://tipulog.co.il/מטפלים-פרטיים — accessed 2026-07-12

This is the direct antithesis of ClinicFlow's positioning (offline, local-first, pay-once). Tipulog is the incumbent to differentiate against on **data sovereignty, no recurring fees, and offline operation** — while being honest that Tipulog beats us on the connected-cloud conveniences listed in Section 5.

## 5. What Tipulog does BETTER than an offline, pay-once product (honesty material)

This section exists to keep our own marketing honest. These are genuine advantages of Tipulog's cloud/subscription model that an offline, local-first, pay-once product (ClinicFlow) **cannot easily match**:

1. **Access from anywhere, any device** — a therapist can open the practice from home, a second clinic, or a phone. Offline/local-install is tied to the machine(s) it's installed on.
2. **Automatic, managed encrypted backups** — no manual backup discipline required from the user. Offline software pushes backup responsibility onto the therapist (a real risk if their disk dies).
3. **Built-in SMS + WhatsApp reminders and confirmations** — reduces no-shows out of the box. An offline product literally cannot send SMS/WhatsApp without an internet path and a messaging provider.
4. **Integrated credit-card payment processing (סליקה)** — collect payment and auto-issue an invoice via SMS link. Hard to replicate offline.
5. **Certified digital invoicing** through INVOICE4U / Green Invoice, plus the tax-authority-registered doctor's journal — turnkey compliance the user doesn't assemble themselves.
6. **Multi-user teams & permissions** — real support for clinics with 2+ staff and per-user access control. Offline single-machine software struggles with concurrent multi-user access.
7. **Google Calendar sync** and questionnaires auto-sent to patients — connected conveniences that assume always-on internet.
8. **Zero setup / no infrastructure**, staffed support center, and continuous server-side updates & new features — nothing for the user to install, patch, or maintain.
9. **Low trial friction** — 30-day free trial, no credit card, no commitment, instant start. A pay-once desktop install has a higher "download + install + license" barrier to first value.
10. **Scales smoothly** from solo therapist to multi-room clinic on the same platform (tiered plans), and now has **larger corporate backing (Arbox)** funding ongoing development.

**Bottom line for positioning:** We win on *no recurring fee, data stays on your machine, works with no internet, no vendor lock-in*. We should NOT claim to beat Tipulog on reminders, payments, multi-device access, or hands-off backups — those are their turf. Lead with sovereignty + total-cost-of-ownership, concede the cloud conveniences gracefully.
