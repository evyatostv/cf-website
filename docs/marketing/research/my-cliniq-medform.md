# Competitor research: MY-CLINIQ + MEDFORM

Research date (all sources accessed): **2026-07-12**
Method: direct fetch of vendor pages (homepage, pricing, security, FAQ, terms-of-use) + web search corroboration incl. one third-party comparison article. Every claim below carries its source URL. Uncertain/conflicting items are flagged inline and collected in the "Uncertainty log" at the end.

---

## 1. MY-CLINIQ (my-cliniq.com)

### Overview
- Israeli SaaS, Hebrew-first, cloud clinic-management platform: "פלטפורמה לניהול קליניקה בענן". Tagline: "לנהל קליניקה, בקלילות".
  Source: https://www.my-cliniq.com/ (accessed 2026-07-12)

### Pricing — VERIFIED
- **₪79/month + VAT per user, single flat price across all tiers.** Confirmed on both the pricing page and FAQ.
  Sources: https://www.my-cliniq.com/pricing/ ; https://www.my-cliniq.com/faqs/ (accessed 2026-07-12)
- Four tiers, same ₪79 unit price, differing by structure (tier names as shown on the pricing page):
  - **פרטי** — solo practitioner, ₪79/mo + VAT. Unlimited active patients, invoices, reminders.
  - **מבוסס** — multi-user clinic (secretary etc.), ₪79/mo **per user** + VAT; permissions, secure internal messaging ("תקשורת פנימית מאובטחת").
  - **מרווח** — multi-room clinic, ₪79/mo **per room** + VAT; "ניהול חדרים ללא הגבלה".
  - **מכון פלוס** — treatment center/institute, ₪79/mo per user + VAT; separate practitioner journals, internal referrals, practitioner finances.
  Source: https://www.my-cliniq.com/pricing/ (accessed 2026-07-12)
- **First month free** ("חודש ראשון במתנה"), no credit card required at signup, no auto-conversion to paid, cancel anytime, no commitment.
  Sources: https://www.my-cliniq.com/ ; https://www.my-cliniq.com/faqs/ (accessed 2026-07-12)
- Annualized cost: **~₪948 + VAT ≈ ₪1,109/year per user, forever.**

### Target professions
- Mental-health-centric: clinical/counseling/rehab psychologists, psychotherapists, couples/family therapists, psychiatrists, clinical social workers; also occupational therapists, nurses, counselors, and physicians (psychiatry, gynecology, plastic surgery, family medicine, dentistry). Dedicated interns program ("My-Cliniq למתמחים"). Institutional plans for multi-provider centers.
  Sources: https://www.my-cliniq.com/ ; https://www.my-cliniq.com/faqs/ (accessed 2026-07-12)

### Features
- Session documentation with templates/questionnaires (claimed 2–3 min per session summary), appointment calendar with room management and **automated SMS reminders**, full patient records (diagnoses, medications), invoicing + expense tracking + tax reports, **funding-bodies (גורמים מממנים) billing/reports**, customizable intake forms, therapy-group management, analytics dashboards, **secured online payment links**, secure internal messaging, letter/document generation, access from any device.
  Sources: https://www.my-cliniq.com/ ; https://www.my-cliniq.com/records ; https://www.my-cliniq.com/calendar ; https://www.my-cliniq.com/invoices/ (accessed 2026-07-12)

### Cloud-only status — VERIFIED cloud-only
- Explicitly "מערכת SaaS ישראלית לניהול קליניקה בענן". **No offline capability or local install mentioned anywhere** (homepage, FAQ, security page). Requires internet.
  Sources: https://www.my-cliniq.com/ ; https://www.my-cliniq.com/faqs/ (accessed 2026-07-12)
- Data storage location / cloud provider / country: **not disclosed** on the site. (Uncertainty U1)
  Source: https://www.my-cliniq.com/security/ (accessed 2026-07-12)

### Security & compliance (vendor claims)
- **ISO 27001** (certified via המכון לבקרה ואיכות) and **ISO 27799** (medical info security, per חוזר מנכ"ל משרד הבריאות). Claims compliance with Israeli Privacy Protection Law and "partial" GDPR compliance. Recurring application penetration tests ("מבחני חדירה אפליקטיביים"). 2FA on unfamiliar devices, auto-logout, encrypted data, automatic backup to separate servers ("מוצפן ומגובה אוטומטית על שרתים נפרדים").
  Sources: https://www.my-cliniq.com/security/ ; https://www.my-cliniq.com/faqs/ (accessed 2026-07-12)

### What MY-CLINIQ does better than ClinicFlow
1. **SMS appointment reminders** — ClinicFlow is strictly offline, no SMS channel at all.
2. **Multi-user with permissions + secure internal staff messaging** — ClinicFlow has no roles/permissions model.
3. **Access from any device / anywhere (web + mobile)** — ClinicFlow is a single-desktop Electron app.
4. **Online payment links to patients** — ClinicFlow has no patient-facing payment collection.
5. **Formal certifications: ISO 27001 + ISO 27799 + MoH-directive alignment** — strong trust signal for institutions; ClinicFlow has no third-party certification.
6. **Automatic off-site backups** — ClinicFlow backups are the user's own responsibility (local).
7. **Funding-bodies (קופות/גורמים מממנים) billing workflow** — not present in ClinicFlow.
8. **Free first month incl. onboarding/training support** — lower-friction trial than a paid one-time license.

## 2. MEDFORM (medform.co.il)

### Overview
- Israeli clinic-management suite marketed as "תוכנת ענן מתקדמת לניהול מרפאה" — EMR + PMS + CRM + marketing in one platform. Mobile app available.
  Source: https://medform.co.il/ (accessed 2026-07-12)

### Pricing — VERIFIED (single plan) with one conflict
- **Single plan: ₪390/month** — "גישה מלאה לפלטפורמה, כל התכונות". Includes **2 users**, EMR, PMS, CRM. VAT treatment not explicitly stated. (Uncertainty U2)
  Source: https://medform.co.il/pricelist/ (accessed 2026-07-12). Corroborated by third-party: https://www.greeninvoice.co.il/magazine/תוכנה-לניהול-מרפאה/ (accessed 2026-07-12) — also says ₪390/mo, 2 users.
- **Storage conflict:** vendor pricelist page showed **100GB**; the Green Invoice article says **8GB**. Likely the vendor page is current and the article stale, but unresolved. (Uncertainty U3)
- **One-time setup fee exists** ("דמי הקמה חד־פעמיים") — amount not published. Data migration and training are paid add-ons. SMS bought as separate credits; payment-processor fees separate. **AI assistant NOT included** in the base plan. (Uncertainty U4 on amounts)
  Source: https://medform.co.il/pricelist/ (accessed 2026-07-12)
- **30-day free trial** ("30 ימים חינם"); monthly billing only, no annual plan, no long-term commitment; cancellation effective end of billing cycle.
  Source: https://medform.co.il/pricelist/ (accessed 2026-07-12)
- **Free tier exists:** homepage: "גישה חינמית למרפאות בתחילת הדרך"; a separate "alpha" page offers full free access during an alpha phase in exchange for feedback, with permanently locked "שותפים לכל החיים" pricing afterward. Whether the alpha program is still open is unclear. (Uncertainty U5)
  Sources: https://medform.co.il/ ; https://medform.co.il/alpha/ (accessed 2026-07-12)
- Annualized cost: **~₪4,680/year** (+ setup fee, + SMS credits, + AI add-on).

### Target professions
- Broad-medical, dental-forward: dental clinics (dedicated module), orthodontists, general physicians, aesthetic/cosmetic clinics, therapists and treatment centers; also names psychologists, physiotherapists, nurses, surgeons, dermatologists, oncologists.
  Sources: https://medform.co.il/ ; https://medform.co.il/dentistry-module/ ; https://medform.co.il/doctor-module/ ; https://medform.co.il/terms-of-use/ ; https://medform.co.il/alpha/ (accessed 2026-07-12)

### Features
- Online patient booking, Google/Outlook calendar sync, automated reminders; EMR with digital forms + e-signatures ("בהתאם להוראות משרד הבריאות" re: signing); **automated WhatsApp/SMS/email**; invoicing, EMV credit-card processing, insurance-claims management, חשבונית ישראל integration, accounting reports; **built-in CRM for leads**; **AI assistant** (add-on); **marketing suite**: automated ad campaigns, built-in SEO, clinic website builder ("אתר תדמית"), Google Business integration, automated Google-review collection; automated medical follow-up module with escalation; workflow automation; **imaging-device/scanner integration** (dental); staff attendance/scheduling; role-based permissions ("הרשאות מבוססות תפקיד"); daily/monthly financial reporting.
  Sources: https://medform.co.il/ ; https://medform.co.il/ai-assistant/ ; https://medform.co.il/built-in-crm/ ; https://medform.co.il/automation/ ; https://medform.co.il/follow-up-module/ ; https://medform.co.il/mini-site/ ; https://medform.co.il/accounting-billing/ ; https://medform.co.il/patient-care/ (accessed 2026-07-12)

### Cloud-only status — NOT strictly cloud-only (important nuance)
- Marketing is cloud-first ("תוכנת ענן") and the cloud version requires internet: "כל עוד קיים חיבור תקין לאינטרנט".
  Sources: https://medform.co.il/ ; https://medform.co.il/faq/ (accessed 2026-07-12)
- **But the terms of use explicitly allow a local install:** "הגישה למערכת תתאפשר באחת משתי הדרכים הבאות: 1. התקנה מקומית של המערכת במחשבי מרפאת הלקוח; 2. גישה מקוונת למערכת" (online.medform.co.il). Under local install, the client bears the legal data-security burden ("על הלקוח חלה חובה לנקוט בכל אמצעי אבטחת המידע המתחייבים לפי דין"). The local option is not marketed anywhere visible; pricing/positioning of it is unknown. (Uncertainty U6)
  Sources: https://medform.co.il/terms-of-use/ ; corroborated by third-party https://badakti.co.il/clinic-management-software/ (both accessed 2026-07-12)
- **Do not claim "MEDFORM is cloud-only" on the ClinicFlow site.** Safe phrasing: "subscription-based, cloud-first" — the subscription applies either way.

### Security & compliance (vendor claims)
- "ענן מאובטח", records encryption, role-based permissions; claims **official Ministry of Health approval**: "מערכת MEDFORM מאושרת רשמית על ידי משרד הבריאות", and Israeli tax-authority approval + חשבונית ישראל integration. The MoH-approval claim appears on the homepage only (not in FAQ) and was **not independently verified** — treat as a vendor marketing claim. (Uncertainty U7)
  Sources: https://medform.co.il/ ; https://medform.co.il/faq/ (accessed 2026-07-12)
- Data storage location / cloud provider / country: not disclosed. (Uncertainty U8)

### What MEDFORM does better than ClinicFlow
1. **Patient communication automations (WhatsApp/SMS/email), online booking, review collection** — impossible in ClinicFlow's strictly-offline model.
2. **Marketing engine**: clinic website builder, SEO, ad campaigns, Google Business — ClinicFlow has none of this.
3. **AI assistant** (documentation/communication/scheduling) — ClinicFlow deliberately has no cloud AI.
4. **CRM/lead pipeline** from inquiry to active patient — ClinicFlow has no lead management.
5. **Payments/insurance**: EMV processing, insurance claims, חשבונית ישראל integration — ClinicFlow doesn't process payments or claims.
6. **Dental depth**: imaging-device integration, dedicated dentistry module — far beyond ClinicFlow's scope.
7. **Multi-user + role-based permissions + staff/attendance management** — ClinicFlow has no roles.
8. **Mobile app + Google/Outlook calendar sync** — ClinicFlow is desktop-only.
9. **Free tier / 30-day trial** — lower entry friction.

## 3. Positioning summary vs ClinicFlow

| | MY-CLINIQ | MEDFORM | ClinicFlow |
|---|---|---|---|
| Price | ₪79/mo + VAT per user (~₪1,109/yr incl. VAT) | ₪390/mo, 2 users (~₪4,680/yr) + setup fee + add-ons | One-time payment |
| Model | Cloud-only SaaS | Cloud-first SaaS; local install exists in ToS but unmarketed | 100% offline desktop |
| Data location | Vendor cloud (provider undisclosed) | Vendor cloud (undisclosed) or clinic PCs (local option) | Clinic's own computer, encrypted |
| Internet required | Yes | Yes (cloud version) | No |
| Target | Mental-health / therapy solo + institutes | Dental + broad medical clinics | Private clinics/therapists (Hebrew, IL) |
| Certifications | ISO 27001, ISO 27799 (claimed) | "MoH approved" (unverified claim) | None (yet) |

**ClinicFlow's wedge holds against both:** no recurring fees (vs ₪948–₪4,680+/yr forever), data never leaves the clinic (Amendment 13 story: the clinic keeps full custody instead of trusting an undisclosed cloud), works with no internet. **Their edge over us:** anything requiring connectivity (SMS/WhatsApp/booking/payments), multi-user roles, mobile access, and formal certifications.

## 4. Uncertainty log
- **U1** — MY-CLINIQ hosting provider/country undisclosed; "Israeli SaaS" per FAQ but server location unknown.
- **U2** — MEDFORM ₪390: VAT-inclusive or not is not stated.
- **U3** — MEDFORM storage: 100GB (vendor pricelist, 2026-07-12) vs 8GB (greeninvoice.co.il article, undated). Conflict unresolved; vendor page presumed current.
- **U4** — MEDFORM setup-fee, migration and training prices not published.
- **U5** — MEDFORM free tier / alpha program: pages exist but current availability and limits unclear.
- **U6** — MEDFORM local-install option: confirmed in ToS + one third-party review, absent from marketing; pricing/nature (on-prem server? sync?) unknown. Avoid the flat claim "MEDFORM is cloud-only".
- **U7** — MEDFORM "מאושרת רשמית על ידי משרד הבריאות" is a homepage marketing claim; no public MoH registry check performed.
- **U8** — MEDFORM hosting provider/country undisclosed.
- **U9** — MY-CLINIQ tier structure (per-user vs per-room by tier) taken from a single fetch of the pricing page; exact tier inclusions worth re-checking before publishing a comparison table.
- All fetched-page summaries were produced by an automated extractor; exact Hebrew wording should be spot-checked before quoting verbatim in public marketing copy.
